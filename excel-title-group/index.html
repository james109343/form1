Excel Title Group

Excel Title Group, LLC is a real estate service provider specializing in protecting and insuring real estate properties from title defects. With offices across Texas, we’re large enough to manage all of your title needs, while serving as a dedicated, qualified resource for every transaction.

From residential and commercial title services to REO/foreclosures and title search products, our family of licensed title professionals serves clients with exceptional, personal treatment. Though we use streamlined methods and innovative technology, our efficiency doesn’t replace our “first-class” service.

At Excel Title Group, we make you feel at home and do all we can to make every transaction as smooth as possible.
