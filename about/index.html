Our Mission

At Clydesdale Title and Escrow, our commitment is to uphold the utmost excellence in real estate dealings. Our achievements are rooted in our promise to consistently provide an exceptional experience, exceeding expectations in residential, commercial, and lender settlement services. We serve as your dependable source for delivering results, without the necessity of physical presence to complete transactions. Contact us ›

Service Excellence

Being knowledgeable and competent means that Clydesdale Title and Escrow can provide excellent service. Our service is built on knowledge, responsiveness, innovation and teamwork. We always seek to develop, implement and provide the most effective real estate settlement resources in the ever-changing, competitive markets of Dallas, Fort Worth and Houston. Contact us ›

Ethics & Integrity

While always finding solutions, Clydesdale Title and Escrow does everything ethically and with integrity. Our principles define who we are as a company and are the reasons why we are asked to represent the best property ownership insurance underwriters in the industry. These underwriters know we do not compromise our fiduciary responsibilities as an independent agency and are committed to closing transactions the right way, while being solution oriented. You can trust Clydesdale Title and Escrow for results. Contact us ›

Dedication

Our team is dedicated to delivering on our promises, getting the right results for our customers, and delivering remarkable service. Clydesdale Title and Escrow promotes an environment of dedicated individuals by building a team of people who are lifelong learners, and embrace innovation, teamwork and sustainability. We are passionate about maintaining our reputation for doing the right thing always, while finding solutions to complex real estate transactions. Clydesdale Title and Escrow is dedicated to the success of our customers through teamwork and keeping our promises. Contact us ›

Dawn Moore
President, AllFirst 

Dawn Moore

President, AllFirst
[email protected]

Dawn Moore is President of AllFirst LLC and CEO of Clydesdale Title and Escrow, a wholly owned subsidiary of AllFirst. Dawn founded Clydesdale Title and Escrow in 2009 and grew it to become one of the largest independent agencies in Texas when it joined AllFirst in 2021. She has been a leader in Texas’ legal and business community for over 38 years serving the real estate and title industry in a variety of capacities. She was one of six lawyers appointed by the State Bar of Texas to the Texas Real Estate Commissions’ Broker/Lawyer Committee, which is responsible for writing the residential purchase/sale contracts used by real estate licensed holders statewide. Dawn served as 2014-2015 President of the Texas Land Title Association and has been a frequent lecturer for the title and mortgage industries and State Bar on real estate matters.

Dawn holds a B.S. degree in Economics, graduating Magna cum Laude and a J.D. degree from Southern Methodist University. In 2005, she was awarded the Distinguished Alumni Award for Corporate Service from the SMU Dedman School of Law. She is Chair Elect of the Dallas Zoo Board of Directors and Co-Chair of the Centennial Master Planning Committee for the City of University Park, Texas.
